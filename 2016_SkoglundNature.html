Changelog:

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de), Clemens Schmid (schmid@shh.mpg.de)

Description: Modern and ancient samples from Southeast Asia and Oceania. This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2)

Literature: Skoglund et al. (2016)

Skoglund, Pontus, Cosimo Posth, Kendra Sirak, Matthew Spriggs, Frederique Valentin, Stuart Bedford, Geoffrey R Clark, et al. 2016. “Genomic Insights into the Peopling of the Southwest Pacific.” Nature 538 (7626): 510–13. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature19844.
