2015_CassidyPNAS

Package version: 2.0.1 (2022-02-21), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Changelog:

  • V 2.0.1: Genetic_Source_Accession_IDs added
  • V 2.0.0: updated the package to Poseidon v2.5 and adjusted the .janno file accordingly

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de)

Description: Neolithic and Bronze Age samples from Ireland. This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2)

Literature: Cassidy et al. (2015)

Cassidy, Lara M., Rui Martiniano, Eileen M. Murphy, Matthew D. Teasdale, James Mallory, Barrie Hartwell, and Daniel G. Bradley. 2015. “Neolithic and Bronze Age Migration to Ireland and Establishment of the Insular Atlantic Genome.” Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 113 (2): 368–73. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1518445113.
