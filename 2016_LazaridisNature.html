2016_LazaridisNature

Package version: 2.0.1 (2022-02-21), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Changelog:

  • V 2.0.1: Genetic_Source_Accession_IDs
  • V 2.0.0: updated the package to Poseidon v2.5 and adjusted the .janno file accordingly

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de), Clemens Schmid (schmid@shh.mpg.de)

Description: Ancient Near Eastern samples from the Mesolithic to the Bronze Age. Also modern reference samples from Europe, the Near East and Northern Africa. This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2)

Literature: Lazaridis et al. (2016)

Lazaridis, Iosif, Dani Nadel, Gary Rollefson, Deborah C Merrett, Nadin Rohland, Swapan Mallick, Daniel Fernandes, et al. 2016. “Genomic Insights into the Origin of Farming in the Ancient Near East.” Nature 536 (7617): 419–24. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature19310.
