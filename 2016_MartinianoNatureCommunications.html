Literature: Martiniano et al. (2016)

Martiniano, Rui, Anwen Caffell, Malin Holst, Kurt Hunter-Mann, Janet Montgomery, Gundula Müldner, Russell L. McLaughlin, et al. 2016. “Genomic Signals of Migration and Continuity in Britain Before the Anglo-Saxons.” Nature Communications 7 (1). https://doi.org/10.1038/ncomms10326.
