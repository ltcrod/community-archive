Olalde, Iñigo, Selina Brace, Morten E. Allentoft, Ian Armit, Kristian Kristiansen, Thomas Booth, Nadin Rohland, et al. 2018. “The Beaker Phenomenon and the Genomic Transformation of Northwest Europe.” Nature 555 (7695): 190–96. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature25738.
