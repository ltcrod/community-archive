2012_PattersonGenetics

Package version: 2.0.0 (2022-01-28), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Changelog:

  • V 2.0.0: updated the package to Poseidon v2.5 and adjusted the .janno file accordingly

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de), Clemens Schmid (schmid@shh.mpg.de)

Description: A large collection of modern samples from all around the world. This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2)

Literature: Patterson et al. (2012)

Patterson, Nick, Priya Moorjani, Yontao Luo, Swapan Mallick, Nadin Rohland, Yiping Zhan, Teri Genschoreck, Teresa Webster, and David Reich. 2012. “Ancient Admixture in Human History.” Genetics 192 (3): 1065–93. https://doi.org/10.1534/genetics.112.145037.
