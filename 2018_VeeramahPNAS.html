Veeramah, Krishna R., Andreas Rott, Melanie Groß, Lucy van Dorp, Saioa López, Karola Kirsanow, Christian Sell, et al. 2018. “Population Genomic Analysis of Elongated Skulls Reveals Extensive Female-Biased Immigration in Early Medieval Bavaria.” Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 115 (13): 3494–9. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1719880115.
