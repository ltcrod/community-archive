Description: This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2). The package contains Iron Age individuals from the Steppe.

Literature: Krzewińska et al. (2018)

Nikitin, Nikolai Shcherbakov, et al. 2018. “Ancient Genomes Suggest the Eastern Pontic-Caspian Steppe as the Source of Western Iron Age Nomads.” Science Advances 4 (10): eaat4457. https://doi.org/10.1126/sciadv.aat4457.
